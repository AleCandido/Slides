## Microcanonical

<span class="hlight green">Energy level sets:</span> $H$ value is <em
class="txtclr blue">conserved</em> by Hamilton's equations

`$$H^{-1}(E) = \{(q,p)| H(q,p)=E\}$$`

They foliate the whole _phase space_ (<span class="txtclr green">$2D-1$</span> sets).
